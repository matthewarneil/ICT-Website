How We Started

In 2021, Blake Thompson and Riley Watson established Hot Beans Web, a web development company, in a bustling Seattle coffee shop. With just their laptops, they created uniquely designed and high-functioning websites for local businesses. Their innovative designs quickly gained recognition, leading to their rapid growth. Today, Hot Beans Web is a global leader in digital solutions, upholding their original philosophy of Great ideas, like hot coffee, are brewed one cup at a time.
